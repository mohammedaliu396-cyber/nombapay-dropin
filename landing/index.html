NombaPay Drop-in

Live · Real Nomba checkout

Payments on any website.
One script tag.

A drop-in Nomba checkout widget for teams who don't want to build a payment UI from scratch. Paste the snippet, pass your amount, and a real Nomba checkout modal opens — Transfer, Card, QR, USSD, Installments, all wired in.

index.html
<script src="nombapay-dropin.js"></script> <script> NombaPayDropin.setup({ amount: 5000, customerEmail: "customer@email.com" }).open(); </script>
